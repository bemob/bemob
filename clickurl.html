Can I see how my landing page is displayed for the visitors?

To check how your landing page opens and displays, you need to access it via campaign link.

This link is listed first in the Campaign Links window which appears right after you finish setting up your campaign on the side of BeMob.

Copy the campaign URL and paste it into your browser.

This is also the link that should be added on the side of your traffic source.

Why do I need a click URL?

The click URL is needed when your campaign has a landing page that is shown before your offer.

Note that a landing page is a separate page that is hosted on your side. It's more commonly named as pre-landing page.

You need to copy the click URL and add it to your CTA button in the code of your landing page.

See our documentation for more examples of how to use a click URL.

Every time visitors click the CTA button on your landing page to go to the offer, the clicks will be registered on the side of BeMob.

You can track the total number of clicks for all and each campaign in particular, as well as see the number of unique, bot and suspicious clicks to further optimize your ad campaigns.

Read about how to do this in our documentation.
